About Thorvarium

Thorvarium is a simple multiplayer game seen in the 90's at one big "portal". Portals at that time were a hipe, great centers of news, gossip and always a section of simple games.

At that time, Globo.com a big corporation had a game called Thorvarium at his portal, and was a hit at that time. Simple, but fun, it is exactly what this project tries to replicate.

The server is build over scala and akka, the continious integratio is handled by travis and the automic deploy results into a server running at heroku and assets deployed at S3.
